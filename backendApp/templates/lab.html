Meet The Team

Our team includes two leading research group in the data science field, one is MIT CSAIL group, and the other is Brown university data management group. We have professor Tim Kraska, PhD student Emanuel Zgraggen and Zeyuan Shang join the team. Together, the team explores and pioneers the breakthrough of the most recent technology in data science.


MIT CSAIL Group

CSAIL have played key roles in developing innovations like the World Wide Web, RSA encryption, Ethernet, parallel computing and much of the technology underlying the ARPANet and the Internet.

webmaster@csail.mit.edu

Brown Data Management Group

This research group is focused on a wide-range of problem domains for database management systems, including analytical (OLAP), transactional (OLTP), machine learning and scientific workloads.

db-www@cs.brown.edu
